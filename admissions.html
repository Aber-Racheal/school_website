Required Documents

1. Birth Certificate

Original and 3 photocopies of child's birth certificate or passport

2. Passport Photos

6 recent passport-size photographs of the child (white background)

3. Previous School Report

For transfers: Progress report from previous school (Primary only)

Copy of parent/guardian National ID or passport

Application Process

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's place at Brights Nursery & Primary School

Step 1: Inquiry & Visit

Contact our admissions office or schedule a school tour. Visit our campus to see our facilities and meet our staff.

Step 2: Collect Application Form

Obtain the application form from the school office or download it from our website. Complete all sections accurately.

Step 3: Submit Documents

Submit the completed form along with all required documents (birth certificate, photos, immunization records, etc.).

Step 4: Assessment & Interview

For Primary applicants, a basic assessment may be conducted. Parent/child interview with the head teacher.

Step 5: Admission Decision

You will receive admission decision within 7 working days. If accepted, you'll receive an admission letter.

Step 6: Payment & Registration

Pay the required fees and complete registration. Collect school uniform and books.
