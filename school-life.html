Transport Services

Modern Fleet
Safety First
Trained Staff

Our Transport Features

Safety Measures:
GPS Tracking First Aid Kits Seat Belts Speed Governors Fire Extinguishers Two-way Radio
Staff Qualifications:
  • All drivers have valid PSV licenses
  • Defensive driving certification
  • First aid and emergency training
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Child protection training
Bus Attendants:

Each bus has a trained attendant to assist students, maintain discipline, and ensure safe boarding/alighting.
