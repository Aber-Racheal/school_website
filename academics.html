Learning Framework

Our Curriculum

At Brights Nursery & Primary School, we follow a comprehensive, child-centered curriculum designed to nurture holistic development. Our approach combines the best practices of early childhood education with Uganda's National Curriculum Framework, ensuring students are well-prepared for their educational journey.

Play-Based

Early Learning

Integrated

Approach

Our Nursery curriculum focuses on play-based learning, social development, and foundational skills. Key areas include:

  • Language and Communication
  • Early Mathematics Concepts
  • Creative Arts and Expression
  • Physical Development
  • Social and Emotional Learning

The Primary curriculum builds on foundational skills with structured academic learning. Core subjects include:

  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics and Science
  • Social Studies
  • Local Language (Luganda)
  • Religious Education
  • Creative Arts and Technology

Curriculum Highlights

Child-Centered Approach

Learning activities tailored to individual needs and developmental stages.

Competency-Based

Focus on developing practical skills and real-world application of knowledge.

Holistic Development

Balancing academic, physical, social, and emotional growth.

Academic Structure

Classes & Sections

Our school is organized into age-appropriate classes with small sections for personalized attention

Nursery Section

Ages 2-5 Years

Classes Offered:
  • Baby Class: Ages 2-3 years 2 Sections (A & B)
  • Middle Class: Ages 3-4 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
  • Top Class: Ages 4-5 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
Maximum 25 students per section

Lower Primary

Primary 1-3

Classes & Sections:
  • Primary One (P1): Age 6-7 years 4 Sections (A, B, C & D)
  • Primary Two (P2): Age 7-8 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
  • Primary Three (P3): Age 8-9 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
Class teacher system with subject specialists

Upper Primary

Primary 4-7

Classes & Sections:
  • Primary Four (P4): Age 9-10 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
  • Primary Five (P5): Age 10-11 years 3 Sections (A, B & C)
  • Primary Six (P6): Age 11-12 years 2 Sections (A & B)
  • Primary Seven (P7): Age 12-13 years 2 Sections (A & B)
Preparation for Primary Leaving Examinations
