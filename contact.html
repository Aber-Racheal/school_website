Frequently Asked Questions

Need Help? Check These FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions about admissions, academics, and school life.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requires a completed application form, birth certificate, passport photos, and immunization records. For transfers, previous school reports are needed. Visit our Admissions page for full details.

What is the school fees structure?

Nursery section is UGX 450,000 per term, Primary section is UGX 550,000 per term. Additional services like transport and lunch are optional. Full fee structure is on the Admissions page.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for outstanding students. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

What are the school hours?

School starts at 7:30 AM and ends at 3:30 PM. Aftercare is available until 5:00 PM for working parents at an additional cost.

Do you provide school transport?

Yes, we have a fleet of school buses covering major routes in Kampala and surrounding areas. Fees range from UGX 150,000 to 200,000 per term.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to visit and see our facilities. Please contact our admissions office to book a tour.
